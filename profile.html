Ellypse Perry

Bio: Hi there! I'm Ellyse, a passionate artist and designer based in beautiful Sydney. With a background in fine arts and a love for experimentation, I specialize in creating vibrant and expressive paintings inspired by nature, culture, and everyday life. My work often explores themes of identity, diversity, and the beauty of imperfection.
